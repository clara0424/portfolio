Hello there! I'm Clara, a sophomore in New York University Abu Dhabi majoring in Interactive Media and minoring in all kinds of things. I've been drawing since as young as I could remember, exploring all kinds of mediums from traditional ink paintings to pencil sketches; however, starting high school, I've began to work mostly with digital illustrations or watercolor and ink paintings. Some of my biggest fields of experience involve graphic design and illustrations using Procreate or Adobe, as well as producing generative art using creative coding platforms. I'm passionate about developing a distinctive art style of my own, and I love using vibrant colors and detailed lines of sketch as well as recreating the nostalgic warmth of hand-drawn illustrations. I've worked as a marketing assistant, IM department's graphic designer, the Gazelle's Multimedia chief editor, a gallerist at the latest Abu Dhabi Arts fair, as well as an intern at SKonec over this summer, a VR game company in Korea. Feel free to explore my journey and accomplishments in the "Projects" window.
